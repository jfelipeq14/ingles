Pronombres:
I: Yo => am

You: Tu => are
He: El => is
She: Ella => is
It: Eso => is

We: Nosotros => are
You: Ustedes => are
They: Ellos => are

Ser/estar => To be

Sujeto - Verbo - Complemento (Afirmativa) + LEOPARD (verb to be)

I am in my house
You are very happy
He is from Italy
She is 20 years old
It is a cat
We are friends
You are good friends
They are in the park

Sujeto - Verbo - Negacion - Complemento (Negativa) + LEOPARD (verb to be)

Contraccion del verbo con la negacion:
I`m not (I)
Isn`t (He, She, It)
aren`t (You, We, You, They)

I`m not in my house
You aren`t very happy
He isn`t from Italy
She isn`t 20 years old
It isn`t a cat
We aren`t friends
You aren`t good friends
They aren`t in the park
